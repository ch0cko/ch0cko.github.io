Welcome to Kurzgesagt

'Kurzgesagt - In a Nutshell' is an animation and production studio that focuses on spreading knowledge in a more digestible form for the average user. With knowledge and information available anywhere these days, they take the time and effort to organize and present that knowledge in an easier manner for their viewers.
